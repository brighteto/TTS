The Early Years

Bright Star Soccer is the result of founder Bright Eto's life long journey as a soccer player. Bright started his playing career at the tender age of three, kicking "sock balls" in his home-country Togo in West Africa.

Fast forward to age 14, Bright and his family moved accross the atlantic to the states. The first couple years were spent in Minnesota where he played club and high school soccer. The family moved further south into the beautiful southern state of North Carolina where Bright finished playing collegiate and semi-professional soccer before heading off to the Netherlands to play professionally.
